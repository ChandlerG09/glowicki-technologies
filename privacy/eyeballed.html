Eyeballed Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

Eyeballed is an online game with accounts and leaderboards, so unlike our on-device apps it stores some data on our servers. Here is exactly what that is. We do not sell your data, run ads, or use third-party analytics.

Account information

When you create an account we store your username, a hashed version of your password, and the country you pick at signup. We do not ask for your email address. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; we cannot read them.

You can also play as a guest with no signup at all. A guest account holds only your gameplay data until you claim it with a username and password.

Gameplay data

To run the game and its leaderboards, we store:

  • Your daily answers and scores
  • Your streak, best streak, and coin balance
  • Your league tier and weekly totals
  • Crews you belong to
  • Items you own and have equipped, such as themes and avatar frames

What other players can see

Leaderboards are the point of the game. Your username, country, avatar frame, and scores appear on the global, country, and league boards. Crew boards are visible only to members of that crew.

Sessions

When you sign in, a session token is stored in your browser to keep you signed in. It is used only for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

Payments

Coin purchases are processed by Stripe. Your card details go directly to Stripe and never touch our servers. We store only what we need to credit your purchase: the coins bought and Stripe's reference for the transaction.

What we do not do

  • No selling or sharing of your data
  • No advertising
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

Deleting your data

Email us with your username and we will delete your account and its data. Unclaimed guest accounts contain nothing that identifies you, but we will delete those on request too.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
