Tend Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

The short version: Tend keeps everything on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your data. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no ads.

Data stored on your device

Tend stores the following data locally on your phone, and nowhere else:

  • The habits you create and your daily check-ins
  • Your plant companion's state and growth
  • Your notification preferences

What we collect

Nothing. Tend has no user accounts, no cloud backup, no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking. We never see your habits and we cannot access them.

Notifications

If you turn on daily reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data leaves your phone.

Purchases

The optional Premium Unlock is a one-time in-app purchase processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see your payment details. RevenueCat, a purchase-management service, processes the purchase receipt from the store to confirm your unlock. RevenueCat receives the purchase token, not your name, payment details, or anything you track in the app.

Deleting your data

Delete the app and your data goes with it. Because everything lives on your device, there is nothing for us to delete on a server.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
