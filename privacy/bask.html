Bask Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

The short version: Bask keeps everything on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your data. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no ads.

Data stored on your device

Bask stores the following data locally on your phone, and nowhere else:

  • The animals you add and their profiles
  • Your logs: feedings, sheds, weight, and enclosure conditions
  • Reminder and notification preferences, if you use Advanced Tracking

If you export an animal's logs to CSV or PDF, the file is created on your device and goes only where you send it.

What we collect

Nothing. Bask has no user accounts, no cloud backup, no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking. We never see your animals' records and we cannot access them.

Purchases

Bask offers one-time pet-capacity unlocks and an optional Advanced Tracking subscription. All purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see your payment details. RevenueCat, a purchase-management service, processes the purchase receipt from the store to confirm your unlock or subscription. RevenueCat receives the purchase token, not your name, payment details, or anything you log in the app.

Deleting your data

Delete the app and your data goes with it. Because everything lives on your device, there is nothing for us to delete on a server.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
