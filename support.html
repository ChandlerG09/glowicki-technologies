Support

We reply within 2 business days, usually sooner.

Every app we make is supported by the people who built it. Email support and put the app name in the subject line so we can help faster.

Eyeballed

The daily visual estimation game at eyeballed.fly.dev. For account issues, purchase problems, or account deletion, email us with Eyeballed in the subject and include your username.

Drive Safe

The BAC and drink tracker for iOS and Android. Email us with Drive Safe in the subject. For purchase issues, the "Restore Purchases" button in the app fixes most unlock problems; if it doesn't, tell us and we'll sort it out.

Tend

The habit companion app. Email us with Tend in the subject. Your data lives on your device, so include your device model and OS version if something looks wrong.

Bask

The reptile husbandry log. Email us with Bask in the subject. Include your device model and OS version for anything technical.

What to include

  • The app name in the subject line
  • What you expected and what happened instead
  • Your device model and OS version, for app bugs
  • A screenshot, if you have one

Refunds

App Store and Google Play purchases are refunded through the store you bought from. Apple users can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com. For Eyeballed purchase problems, email us directly.
