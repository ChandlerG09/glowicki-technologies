# Glowicki Technologies — Business Site

The public business site for Glowicki Technologies. Static HTML/CSS/JS, no build step.

## Purpose

- Business presence for Plaid verification, App Store review, and general legitimacy
- Showcase of shipped products (Eyeballed, Drive Safe, Tend, Bask)
- Privacy policies and terms for every app (required by app stores)
- Contact point: chandlerglowicki.professional@gmail.com

## Structure

- `index.html` — main site
- `privacy/<app>.html` — per-app privacy policies (linked in store listings)
- `terms.html` — terms of use
- `support.html` — support page (App Store support URL)

python3 -m http.server 8080
